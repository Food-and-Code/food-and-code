The People of F&C

Yep, the myth is true: F&C happens every now and then when angels and demons get bored. Here are a few of the spotted faces.

The Core Team: spirit of F&C

Those without whom it wouldn't happen. Or at least not now.

Roman Miletitch Technological artist, PhD student at IRIDIA in artificial intelligence, community/event organiser. Discovering first coding and then art, Roman is highly interested in the writing of code as a creative process and the liberty that emerges when a programming language is seen as any other language. Lately, he's been seen working on movement based interactive pieces and a crazy machin called Flippaper.
Email roman@opentechschool.org
Website aporagen.com

Alicia March Dandy catwoman over which Batman has no power. Futured curator of the year by The Time in 2020 (we'll be there to check), Alicia makes good use of her 9 lives, which still doesn't explain how she has time to do all she does. Coaching our dear kittens, putting her scenography skills to good use, dancing all night long, count on her to embody the spirit of F&C from dawn to dawn.
